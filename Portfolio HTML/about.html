Hello, my name is John Swanson.

I am a recent graduate of North Dakota State University with a double major in mathematics and chemistry.

I have loved photography since I got my first point-and-shoot camera at age 10. I continued engaging this art until I started taking pictures for a student newspaper upon coming to college. This eventually progressed to becoming photo editor for two years.

Photography continues to be a passion of mine. This site serves as an archive of all the photos I have never been able to share, and of some that I have.

I currently shoot with a Nikon D600. My go to lens is the nifty-fifty, but I have been loving my 80-200mm f/2.8 as of late.
