Hello, my name is John Swanson.

I am a graduate of North Dakota State University with a double major in mathematics and chemistry.

I have loved photography since I got my first point-and-shoot camera at age 10. I continued engaging this art until college, where I took pictures for a student newspaper, which progressed to being photo editor.

Photography continues to be a passion of mine. This site serves as an archive of all the photos I have never been able to share and of some that I have. I currently shoot with a Nikon D550. My go to lens is the nifty-fifty, but I have been loving my 80-200mm f/2.8 as of late.
